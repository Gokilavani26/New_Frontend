Data Annotation Service

AI-Powered methodologies for Data Annotation

The types of data annotations in machine learning, we offer:

  • Image Annotation: This involves labeling objects, features, and regions in images, such as bounding boxes, semantic segmentation, and object detection.
  • Annotation: This involves labeling text data with relevant categories, such as sentiment analysis, entity recognition, and part-of-speech tagging.
  • Audio Annotation: This involves labeling audio data, such as speech recognition, transcription, and speaker identification.
  • Video Annotation: This involves labeling objects, features, and regions in videos, such as action recognition, object tracking, and scene detection.
